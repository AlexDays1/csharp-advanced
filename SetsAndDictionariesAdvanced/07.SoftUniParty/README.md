1. 7.SoftUni Party

#### There is a party in SoftUni. Many guests are invited and there are two types:  VIP and regular. When guest come check if he/she exists in any of the two reservation lists.

#### All reservation numbers will be with 8 chars.

#### All VIP numbers start with a digit.

#### There will be 2 command lines. First is &quot;PARTY&quot; - party is on and guests start coming. Second is &quot;END&quot; – then party is over, and no more guest will come

#### Output has to be all the guests, who didn&#39;t come to the party (VIP must be first) and their count.
